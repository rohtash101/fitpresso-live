Privacy Policy Of FitSpresso

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information collected by FitSpresso, how it is used, and the steps we take to protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

FitSpresso follows standard procedures for using log files, which are common practice among hosting providers. These log files track visitors to the website and capture certain data including:

IP Addresses
Browser Type
Internet Service Provider (ISP)
Date and Time Stamp
Referring/Exit Pages
Number of Clicks
This information is used solely for analyzing trends, managing the site, tracking users’ movement on the website, and gathering demographic data. None of this data is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like most websites, FitSpresso uses cookies to enhance the user experience. Cookies store information such as:

Visitors' preferences

Pages viewed on the website
This data helps us optimize the user experience by customizing content based on your browser type and other preferences. Cookies can also help us track which pages are visited most often.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses DART cookies to serve ads on FitSpresso. These cookies allow Google to serve ads based on users' visits to our site and other sites on the internet.

Privacy Policies of Third-Party Advertisers

FitSpresso works with third-party advertising networks that use cookies, JavaScript, and web beacons to serve targeted ads. These technologies automatically collect information, including your IP address, when you interact with their ads.

It’s important to note that FitSpresso does not have access to or control over the cookies used by third-party advertisers.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for detailed information on their practices. You can learn how to opt-out of certain options by visiting their respective websites. For your convenience, you can view a list of these privacy policies and their links here: [Privacy Policy Links].

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. For more information on how to disable cookies in specific browsers, please visit the help section of your browser's website.

Children’s Privacy

FitSpresso prioritizes the protection of children’s privacy online. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities.

FitSpresso does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us immediately. We will take all necessary steps to remove such information from our records.

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies only to online activities conducted on our website. It does not extend to information collected offline or via other channels outside of this website.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.

Updates

FitSpresso reserves the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be reflected at the top of the document.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or concerns regarding how we collect and use your information, please feel free to contact us.
